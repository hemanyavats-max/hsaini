About me

Hemanya Saini -
engineering with precision

As a first-year Electronics and VLSI Engineering student at VIT Vellore, I thrive at the intersection of hardware and software — equally comfortable synthesising RTL on an FPGA as I am pitching to a non-technical audience. My work spans real-time ECG arrhythmia detection, Morse code transceivers in Verilog, and network anomaly dashboards in Python. Beyond the lab, I serve as VP of Substantive Affairs at VITMUNSOC, leading research and debate training for a 2,000-member society — which has sharpened my critical thinking, communication, and cross-functional leadership. I am actively seeking opportunities in technology, product, or operations where analytical rigour and strong communication can drive real-world impact.
